# 30-Second “Tell Me About Yourself” –  

> “I’m Rohit Vishwakarma from Mumbai, completing my **B.Sc. in 2025 from the University of Mumbai**. I’m a **Backend and Full-Stack Developer** with experience in **Node.js, Express.js, Django, PostgreSQL**, and **React.js**.

## Tips for Delivery

1. Speak **confidently and slowly**; don’t rush.  
2. Keep it **1–2 minutes long**.  
3. Highlight **projects, skills, and internship**—these are your strongest points.  
4. End with **enthusiasm for the role**.
